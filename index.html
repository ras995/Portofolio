VIDEO

My last team project was to create a video that describes step by step the process of making an origami. The origami is making a comeback. It is the ancient art of paper folding, and it has applications in the modern-day classroom for teaching geometry, thinking skills, fractions, problem solving, and fun science. Our video goal to be both informative and enjoyable at the same time.
